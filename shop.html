Shopping with Art & Collectibles


To purchase an item on Art & Collectibles:


  • Find an item you want to purchase.
  • Click Buy Now.
  • Click Proceed to checkout.
  • If you’re signed in to your Art & Collectibles account, confirm your shipping address and payment details.
  • Review your order to check that your information and order are correct.

Congratulations!!

Art & Collectibles will send you an email for each item that you’ve purchased. If you have any questions about your order, be sure to reach out to us directly via social media accounts or leave us a message in the contact us section.


Cart & Payment


What Payment Methods Can I Use to Check Out on Art & Collectibles?


  • Credit cards
  • Esewa
  • COD

Credit and debit cards

We accept credit and debit cards on Art & Collectibles accept most major credit issuers, including: Visa Mastercard/Eurocard You may see a pending transaction on your credit card bill for a small amount. This is a pre-authorization test to ensure your card is valid. Once your card has been validated, it will be dropped from your statement.


Esewa Pay

Esewa Pay is a mobile payment service available to many buyers who are shopping on the mobile app or Safari on desktop or mobile.


COD

You might as well order the products online and pay during delivery.
